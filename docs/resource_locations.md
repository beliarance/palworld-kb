# Palworld 1.0 — Resource Map: Where to Mine & Gather What

Knowledge-base note: best farming spots and dedicated-base locations per key resource.
Game version: **Palworld 1.0** (released 10 July 2026). Node locations verified only in
Early-Access-era guides are marked **(pre-1.0 guide)** — EA spots are mostly still valid in 1.0,
and 1.0-updated guides re-confirm the flagship spots. New 1.0 content is marked **(PRELIMINARY 1.0)**.
Coordinates are in-game map (x, y). Machine-readable version: `data/resource_nodes.json`.

## 1. Ore — the classic "ore base"

| Spot | Coords | Nodes | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Verdant Brook peak** (NE of Sealed Realm of the Guardian) | (190, -40) | ~8 Ore + ~6 Coal | **THE meta spot, 1.0-confirmed.** Summit raids can't path to ("unraidable"); ore + coal together = Refined Ingots on site. Also cited as (189, -37)/(192, -37) — same summit. |
| Verdant Brook ore mine | (270, -230) | ~8 Ore | Dedicated mining outpost; plateau at (270, -215) adds ~6 Coal (1.0 guide). |

| West of Fort Ruins | (156, -396) | — | Classic early ore + wood. (pre-1.0 guide) |
| SW of Small Settlement | (8, -528) | — | Huge flat area + lots of ore — best "big main base with ore". (pre-1.0 guide) |
| South of Cinnamoth Forest | (-77, -310) | — | Flat and spacious; ore + wood, sulfur nearby. (pre-1.0 guide) |

**Base verdict:** dedicated ore base is still worth it. Best pick = Verdant Brook peak (190, -40)
as the **coal+ore base** (see §2). Note: the placeable **Ore Mining Site** (early tech) lets a main
base self-supply ore late-game; node clusters remain faster for bulk.

## 3. Pure Quartz — the "quartz base" (Astral Mountains, far north)

| Spot | Coords | Nodes | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Pristine Snow Field** | (-213, 250) | ~5–6 | Flat field, dense nodes right by fast travel — easiest run. (pre-1.0 guide) |
| Below PAL Genetic Research Unit Tower entrance | (-257, 391) | cluster | Rich veins along the ridges. (pre-1.0 guide) |
| Land of Absolute Zero | (-417, 471) | 4–6 | High cliffside; aggressive high-level Pals. (pre-1.0 guide) |
| Near Unthawable Lake | (-354, 296) | 1+ | Chunk near an ore node, steps from the travel point. (pre-1.0 guide) |

Cold-resistant gear + **Mining Lv 3** Pals required. **Base verdict:** Pristine Snow Field is the
classic quartz base, but the **Pure Quartz Mine** placeable (Feybreak-era; costs 100 Pure Quartz)
means you only need to hand-mine the first ~100 — then automate at any base.

## 5. Paldium Fragment — no base needed

- Starter spots: **Northern Plateau of Beginnings (233, -488)** and **Southern (264, -548)** —
  paldium + stone/wood/ore, next to a mineable dungeon. (pre-1.0 guide)
- Blue-veined rocks concentrate **near water** (rivers, coasts). Cliffs west of the starting beach:
  20+ deposits on a ~5-min loop, ~15-min respawn.
- **The real answer is the Crusher** (Lv 8, Watering Pal): Stone → Paldium Fragments at any base,
  infinitely; **Refrigerated Crusher** (Lv 52) scales it. Never build a base for paldium.

## 6. Crude Oil — the "oil base" + oil rigs

- Oil fields (dark patches) are mined with the **Oil Extractor** placed on the patch, powered by
  generators + Electricity Pals (**Orserk** is the fastest).
- **Best oil base: Sakurajima NW (-646, 270)** — large flat area over an oil field; 1.0 guide calls
  it "the definitive Crude Oil base". Alternatives on Sakurajima: central (-552, 192), east
  (-493, 109). (coords pre-1.0 guide except NW, re-confirmed 1.0)
- Combo option: Dessicated Desert oil field **(289, 243)** — 2 oil spots + 7 coal nodes. (pre-1.0 guide)
- **Oil rigs (raid content, not bases):** starter barge ~Lv 30 near spawn; **Oil Rig Stronghold
  Lv 55 at ~(608, -435)**, SE of Marsh Island — big yellow chest (random spot each in-game day)
  gives ~200 Crude Oil + Ancient Civilization Cores + legendary schematics; guarded by anti-air
  missiles, approach low/by water. Third rig: Rayne Syndicate Platform (Lv 50+). In 1.0 the rigs'
  enemy-controlled Pals are capturable. (PRELIMINARY 1.0)

## 7. Chromite & Hexolite Quartz (Feybreak)

- **Chromite is buried** — find it with the **Metal Detector** (30 Plasteel, 100 Paldium Fragment,
  30 Circuit Board, 20 Nightstar Sand @ Production Assembly Line II) or with **Smokie**'s partner
  skill (Dark Pal, northern Feybreak = a living detector). Dig in the **red zones** on the Feybreak
  map. (pre-1.0 guide)
- Reliable repeat spawn: **cave at ~(-1200, -1100)**; the four Feybreak Alpha arenas each host
  multiple Chromite nodes — run them as a circuit. (pre-1.0 guide)
- **Hexolite Quartz** field at **(-1066, -1430)**. Recipe: 5 Chromite + 12 Hexolite Quartz +
  20 Ore → 1 **Hexolite** (Gigantic Furnace, Tech Lv 56). (pre-1.0 guide)
- **Base verdict:** no classic node-camp — Chromite is a detector circuit. Outpost near the quartz
  field only if smelting on Feybreak.

## 8. Ancient Civilization Parts

Not a map node. Sources: **field Alpha bosses** (drop + respawn — farm one repeatedly), dungeon
bosses, **Lucky Pals**, rare chests, Pal Expeditions. Easiest early farm: cycle the ~Lv 13 dungeons
on the starter island.

## 9. World Tree — 1.0 endgame zone (PRELIMINARY 1.0)

Access: complete **Sunreach** (unlocked by beating Bjorn & Bastigor at Feybreak Tower, then
disabling six defense modules). Zone facts: Lv 75+ enemies; radiation pockets need the **Gas Mask**;
Teafant Spring statues heal you and your party Pal.

**The vanish rule:** Paloxite, Mythical Wood, Radiant Gems and Ancient Relic drops **disappear when
approached** unless you (a) drink/apply **World Tree Holy Water** on yourself AND your active Pal
(drops from zone mobs, slow to farm), or (b) run a Pal with the **Dimensional Leap** passive
(+50% move speed, +15% hunger drain, negates vanishing) — hatch **Large Ominous Eggs** found in the zone.

- **Paloxite** (mine with Plasma Multicutter → Paloxite Ingots, top-tier gear/structures):
  - 2 clusters at the zone center by the **giant orange crystal** (one in front, one just west);
  - **5 nodes near Remnant Riverside** fast travel — safest loop: teleport, mine, deposit;
  - 3 + 2 nodes near **Gilded City Ruins Watchtower** fast travel, at varying heights — bring a flyer.
  - No numeric coordinates published yet (zone appears to use its own sub-map).
- **Radiant Gems**: scattered elemental drops across the World Tree biomes. Combine element-matched
  gems into **Awakening Gems** → use on a same-element Pal to unlock its Awakening stat boost.
  Bundle gem runs with Paloxite + Mythical Wood farming.
- **Mythical Wood**: glowing trees throughout the zone, same vanish rule. Used with Paloxite for
  endgame gear (e.g. Wing Pack).
- **Ancient Civilization Relic**: drops from Pals defeated in the zone; also hard-mode tower bosses,
  zone fishing, locked chests, and the **"World Tree's Forbidden Area" Pal Expedition**. Feed stacks
  into the **Ancient Relic Recycler** at your main base — it outputs a steady stream of every World
  Tree material (the lazy way to get Paloxite/Gems/Wood).

**Base verdict:** you don't base in the World Tree — farm loops from fast travel points; build the
Ancient Relic Recycler at home.

## 10. Sunreach / Sky Islands (PRELIMINARY 1.0)

- **Soralite** — Sunreach-exclusive: huge boulders ringed by small blue crystals, mined with the
  **Plasma Multicutter**, smelted into **Soralite Ingots** for advanced 1.0 gear. Known cluster on an
  open ridge at **(583, 144)** (1.0 guide) — small outpost there is worthwhile for bulk crafting.
- Other 1.0-guide base spots: Flame Pulse Island flat zone (788, 457); "Foot of the World Tree"
  lakeside (841, 639, needs aquatic build kit); open-water builds now possible (1.0 aquatic
  construction).

## Quick answer: the classic dedicated-base set

1. **Main / coal+ore base:** Verdant Brook unraidable peak **(190, -40)** — 8 Ore + 6–8 Coal.
2. **Big production base:** SW of Small Settlement **(8, -528)** or open meadow (-35, -170) — flat space + ore.
3. **Sulfur base:** Mount Obsidian west **(-435, -530)** or Eternal Pyre **(-595, -520)**.
4. **Quartz base:** Pristine Snow Field **(-213, 250)** — until the Pure Quartz Mine is up.
5. **Oil base:** Sakurajima NW **(-646, 270)** + raid the Lv 55 Oil Rig **(608, -435)**.
6. Late game, placeable mines (Ore/Coal/Sulfur/Quartz) let you consolidate — keep the oil base;
   everything else can move home.
